\hypertarget{introduction}{%
\section{Introduction}\label{introduction}}

This reports compares the ALSI (J200) and the SWIX (J400) top 40
Indexes. The SWIX Top 40 consists of the 40 most investable companies in
the SWIX All Share Index (J403). The J400's weighting methodology is a
SWIX free float market cap weighted. The J200 consists of the 40 most
investable companies in the All Share Index (J203). The J200's weighting
methodology is also the free float market cap weighted.
